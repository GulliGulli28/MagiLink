About us

We are fans of Harry Potter World

We want people that share the same passion than us to meet, chat and share wonderfull moments. With MagiLink, it is possible to find people both from your same house (Griffindor, Ravenclaw, Hufflepuff and Slytherin) or not, and that are close to you. There are weekly activities, where the different houses are in competition. You will find different games, quizz etc.. to show if you are a real Harry Potter fan!
